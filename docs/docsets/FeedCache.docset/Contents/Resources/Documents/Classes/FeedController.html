FeedController

public class FeedController <T:FeedItem> : FeedControllerGeneric

The FeedController keeps track of incoming feed items and caches them accordingly. It is designed to be controlled by a UITableViewController or UICollectionViewController, and is modeled after the NSFetchedResultsController.
