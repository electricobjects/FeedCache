  • FeedItem

    Your item models should follow this protocol, impelmenting Hashable and NSCoding. The FeedController compares incoming items to the ones already present, integrating new ones while eliminating redundant ones. It must compare the items using the hashValue in order to determine what items are new and which ones are not.
